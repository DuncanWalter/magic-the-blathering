Magic The Gathering Card Collector V0.0.8

This app was created with the intention of providing Magic the Gathering players a way to organize and keep digital collections of their cards in a way that they could be easily filtered and searched. Our application is designed to allow a user to create collections, add or remove cards, and move them around their created collections.
We also designed this application with store owners in mind who may wish to keep a digital inventory of cards available in the store for quick customer interaction and lookup times.
